These first two charts are an early warning signal of an upcoming recession. Inversions in the yield curve have preceded every recession since the 1950's.

It is frequently suggested that global economies take their lead from the US. Wall St and US economic data seem to set the tone for every other market. In 2022 US GDP was worth 25.32% of the world economy. In 2023 that number rose to 25.95%. And as of 2025 1 out of every 4 consumer dollars spent in the world is spent in the US. If the US is so important then it may be useful to look at some key statistics.

In my opinion the 5 main indicators of US economic health are 1. Interest Rates, 2. Unemployment, 3. Inflation, 4. GDP, and 5. Exchange Rate
